Heidi's French Toast Recipe

Heid's french toast is delicious! Trust me, you want to guess correctly!

  • Serves: 4
  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread
  • syrup
  • butter
  • love (optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in a bowl.
  2. Pour in milk.
  3. Dip one slice of bread in mixture, covering both sides.
  4. Cook in a skillet on medium heat until both sides are golden brown.
  5. Repeat for remaining 3 slices of bread.
  6. Top with butter and syrup.
  7. Enjoy!
